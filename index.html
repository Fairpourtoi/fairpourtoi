About

Fair Pour Toi.
/fair · pour · twa/

It means for you in French — and that’s exactly the point.

Fair Pour Toi brings private, appointment only resale events to Santa Monica. Offering a variety of contemporary and luxury pieces where you can shop at your own pace, curated just for you.

Our Mission

We built Fair Pour Toi around a simple idea: resale should feel considered, not chaotic. Away from the noise of warehouse sales and traditional retail, we connect sellers directly with people who care about quality. Every piece is hand-selected for its craftsmanship and lasting style, not just for the label.

Our mission is simple: an exclusive, focused edit that honors both the garment and the person who wears it next.
