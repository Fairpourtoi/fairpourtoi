About

Fair Pour Toi.
/fair · pour · twa/

It means for you in French — and that’s exactly the point.

Fair Pour Toi brings private, appointment only sales & events to Santa Monica. Offering a variety of contemporary and luxury pieces where you can shop at your own pace, curated just for you.

Our Mission

We built Fair Pour Toi around a simple idea: sales should feel considered, not chaotic. Away from the noise of warehouse sales and traditional retail, we connect sellers directly with people who care about quality. Every piece is hand-selected for its craftsmanship and lasting style, not just for the label.

Our mission is simple: an exclusive, focused edit that honors both the garment and the person who wears it next.

For Designers & Brands

A Considered Home

We partner with designers and brands to move sample sale, clearance, and end-of-season inventory. We do so discreetly, thoughtfully, and without compromising how your brand is perceived.

Brand Integrity, Protected
No public markdown racks or discount signage. Your pieces are presented with the same care as our curated sales — never positioned as overstock.
A True Boutique Setting
Private, appointment-only events in Santa Monica, not a warehouse liquidation. Shoppers experience your pieces the way they were meant to be seen.
A Personal Experience
Every guest is guided through the collection one-on-one while sipping champagne, trying pieces on and hearing the story behind them.
Reach Beyond the Room
Pieces that don't sell in person continue their story online at shop.fairpourtoi.com thereby extending your reach without ever feeling like a clearance bin.
